CA11y Project

CA11y (Content Accessibility: Highly Individualised Digital Content for Supporting Diverse Needs) is funded through an EPSRC New Investigator Award (EP/X012395/1).

The project is led by Principal Investigator Dr Timothy Neate (Department of Informatics, King’s College London) and Co-Investigator Dr Madeline Cruice (Department of Language and Communication Science, City, University of London) and will run from September 2023 to September 2025.
