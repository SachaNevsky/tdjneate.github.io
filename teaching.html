PhD Student Recruitment

I will soon be recruiting a PhD student to work with me at the Human-Centred Computing group in King's College London, to begin around October 2021. This will be a fully-funded PhD (fees and stipend) and you will recieve a good budget for equipment and travel. I am looking for applicants who can demonstrate strong technical skills (building functional applications, machine learning, hardware) and can show experience in human-centred computing to do ambitious and impactful user-centred work in healthcare/accessibility.

Get in touch if you want to discuss ideas for potential projects or want to find more about doing a PhD in general:
