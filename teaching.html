Teaching and Supervision Experience



Current Teaching and Supervision

I am a visiting lecturer and am happy to discuss supervision of any projects with MSc students at City, UoL. I am currently keen to supervise projects on:

  • Projects which look to enable people to be creative (could be writing, music, art). I am particularly interested in thinking about people with aphasia.

  • Tangible or tactile user-interfaces.

  • Multi-device computing, especially around television and media contexts.



Previous Experience

Demonstrating and Teaching

I have done a mixture of demonstrating and guest teaching for the following modules: Mobile Interaction Design, Introduction to Human-Computer Interaction, Inclusive Design, Python Programming, Algorithms and Automata, C++ Programming, Java Programming, Introduction to Human-Computer Interaction. I have also marked a number of BSc Projects and MSc projects. Let me know if you require more detail.

I have also successfully supervised a number of MSc projects in Human-Computer Interaction at Swansea and City, University of London. These have ranged in scope widely from user research, to technical implmentation-based projects. Please get in contact if you would like more information about previous projects.
