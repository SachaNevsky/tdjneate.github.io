Teaching and Supervision Experience



Current Teaching and Supervision

I am a visiting lecturer and am happy to discuss supervision of any projects with MSc students at City, UoL. I am interested in a range of topics in HCI. Look at my Videos or Publications to get an idea of my interests. Contact me with your interests, or more concrete project plans around the following topics if you are stuck for a starting point:

  • Projects which look to enable people to be creative (could be writing, music, art).

  • Multi-device computing when users interact with mobile devices while watching television.

  • Projects with mid-air haptics such as Ultraleap (we have Ultraleap).



Previous Experience

Demonstrating and Teaching

I have done a mixture of demonstrating and teaching for the following modules: Mobile Interaction Design, Introduction to Human-Computer Interaction, Inclusive Design, Python Programming, Algorithms and Automata, C++ Programming, Java Programming, Introduction to Human-Computer Interaction, Inclusive Design I have also marked several BSc Projects and MSc projects. Let me know if you require more detail.

I have also successfully supervised a number of MSc projects in Human-Computer Interaction at Swansea and City, University of London. These have ranged in scope widely from user research to technical implementation-based projects. Please get in contact if you would like more information about previous projects.
