Liquid Metal Interfaces

While a Research Officer at Swansea University I conducted research into how we might use liquid metal as a programmable material. Inspired by Ishii's notion of tangible bits, we focused on how one might programmably actuate materials as output. For this project I iteratively and built a number of liquid-metal interfaces, with corresponding control algorithms.

Multi-Device Media

For my PhD I worked with the BBC to understand and consider how we might design for the second screen use-case: basically, the concept of engaging with one's mobile device while watching television. My main interest was to consider how we might use technological interventions to optimise the a user's attention focus between the two screens.
